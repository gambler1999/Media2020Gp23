The right way to learn

In the concept section, you can select the chapter you want to learn and click the left mouse button to enter the page of the corresponding section. Here is the principle and theoretical knowledge, slide the scroll down to get the content outside the current screen.

In the experiment section, you can click the left mouse button to select the experiment you want to simulate. We prepared three basic experiments.

  • In the coin toss experiment, you can choose the number of coin tosses and click the "begin trial" button to get the experiment results.
  • In the Ball Fetch experiment, click the "Take One" button, the system will randomly draw a small ball from the black box, click this button multiple times to draw multiple small balls without putting back. Click the "Reset" button to reset the experiment.
  • In the Normal Distribution experiment, click the "start" button, and the function image will change according to the change of m. Click the "Stop" button, the value of function and m will stop changing.

In the Assement section, you can click the dot in front of the option to complete the answer. There is a "check" button under each question so that you can submit answers and view the correct answers and explanations
